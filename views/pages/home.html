About server

Server is based on the epic story of Knights and Marigold!

Step into the captivating world of "Between Knights and Marigold," where two factions clash in a struggle for dominance and side stories.
Choose your allegiance as a valiant Knight or enigmatic Marigold supporter, with nine charismatic leaders on each side, each possessing unique traits and abilities.
Engage in thrilling discussions about anime, manga, novels, games, and movies with like-minded enthusiasts.
Participate in quizzes, gaming sessions, and unlock exclusive content related to the epic tale. Embrace a friendly and inclusive atmosphere where imagination thrives, friendships bloom, and creativity shines.
